To Parents, Caregivers, and Volunteers:

Dear All:

You are without a doubt your child’s most important teacher. Your on-going investment will prepare them for a world that was very different from the one we grew up in. The Internet, which either did not exist or was in its infancy when we were our children’s age, has created an intimate global village where, for a very low cost can talk to our fellow human on any one of the seven continents. For some this is very exciting and almost unlimited opportunities, while for others it is a source of fear, uncertainty, and anxiety.

Although we cannot put the Technology Genie back in the bottle, we can do all in our power to prepare ourselves and our children to.

Newark Kids Code is not just a coding program. Rather, it is a community of like-minded and forward-thinking people committed to creating a culture around technology and committed to the fundamental proposition that given access to the proper tools and resources our children can compete with their counterparts anywhere in the world.

As a parent of a technologically underserved child, we invite you to join our movement to create the next generation of youth that will be creators and not just passive users of technology and use their newly-acquired “Superpowers” to make the world a better place.

Click on the “Get Involved” link to join our movement today!

Best Regards,
Darrin S. Sharif
Director of Newark Kids Code
